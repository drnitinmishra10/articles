Chapter 1: The First Code

The University Years with Maya

During his university years, Arjun's world of structured code was first disrupted by love. This chapter explores his relationship with Maya, a bright-eyed engineering student who introduced him to a world beyond algorithms. Their bond was one of innocent firsts, teaching Arjun the language of human connection, only for him to learn his first lesson in heartbreak.

"You're a brilliant chapter, Arjun, but I'm looking for a different book."

- Maya

Chapter 2: The Logic Gate

The PhD Journey with Sonia

In his postgraduate years, Arjun sought refuge in his research, becoming more guarded. He met Sonia, a fellow PhD student whose intellect matched his own. Theirs was a love built on mutual respect and shared ambition. This section examines how their logical, intellectual bond was ultimately severed by the cold, hard logic of career and opportunity.

"This new man is a more 'efficient' partner for my professional goals."

- Sonia

Chapter 3: The Debugging Process

The Professor's Tenure with Neha

As a young professor, Arjun had achieved professional success but was emotionally isolated. He met Neha, a vibrant artist who brought chaos and color into his structured life. This chapter explores their passionate, whirlwind romance and how the very spontaneity that drew them together ultimately proved incompatible with Arjun's ordered world.

"You live in a perfect box, Arjun, but I need to live in the open air."

- Neha

Chapter 4: The Final Error

The Mentor's Fall with Kavya

An established and respected mentor, Arjun had all but given up on love. His final relationship was with Kavya, a brilliant student whose admiration made him feel truly seen. This chapter details how this seemingly perfect connection was a facade for ambition, delivering the most devastating blow and forcing Arjun to confront the fundamental flaw in his understanding of the heart.

"You’ve taught me everything I needed to know, Professor."

- Kavya

Conclusion: The Unsolvable Equation

Arjun's life has been a series of open-ended loops, each ending in a syntax error he couldn’t anticipate. This final visualization compares the dynamics of all four relationships, revealing the patterns of his heartbreak. He is left to ponder his own algorithm, wondering if it's possible to write a new one for himself.
